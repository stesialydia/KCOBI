GET TO KNOW KCOBI

KCOBI foods limited is a local ARGO production industry located in the heart of Aba,which processes agricultural product into finished food for easy family consumption. Our raw materials are rice, beans, corn and cassava which goes through processing, packaging, and distribution/sales of processed agricultural foods.

KCOBI manufacturing priorities are:

  • To ensure a quality distinctive taste while ensuring the processed crops do not loose its nutritional value.
  • Maintaining a good hygiene.
  • Quality and standard worthiness of a nation export.
  • Guarantees customer satisfaction from the retailers down to our consumers.
  • Lastly, it has NAFDAC approval.
