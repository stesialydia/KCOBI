INTRODUCTION TO KCOBI

The Demand for easy to prepare meal consumption in Nigeria has been continuously increasing from a modest average annual per capital consumption.This rising demand is mainly a consequence of the fast progression of urbanization and Kcobi Foods is here to deliver on that demand with various brands of processed crops like bean flour made from beans grain and can be used for moi moi and akara. Plantain flour is made from unripe plantain. sunvita is a low sugar cassava product which is prepared and served with soup. papvita is a mazie product. And a host of others and more to come.

GOAL

Our goal is to ensure that KCOBI is the number one meal on every family table, occassions and events with its distinctive refined taste that sets Kcobi products apart from the rest.
